🧠 Spaced Repetition Settings

Customise how the SM-2 algorithm schedules your card reviews. Cards are shown at increasing intervals — the goal is to review each card just before you would forget it.

How it works

After you reveal a card you rate your recall quality. The algorithm uses that rating to schedule the next review:

Button What it means Effect on interval
Again Forgot completely or never seen Reset — review again tomorrow
Hard Recalled, but barely — significant effort Interval grows slowly
Good Recalled correctly after brief hesitation Standard interval increase
Easy Recalled perfectly, no hesitation Larger interval increase

The first two reviews of a new card come after 1 day then 6 days. After that, intervals grow by a multiplier (the easiness factor, starting at 2.5) that adjusts with each review.
